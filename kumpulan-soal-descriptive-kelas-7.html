Descriptive Text

Pantai Galesong

Pantai Galesong is located in Makassar, South Sulawesi. It is 10 km away from Losari Beach and you will need 30 minutes by car to reach the place.

Even though Pantai Galesong is quite close to Pantai Losari, but it is not included in Makassar’s tourism destination. Instead, it is part of the Talakar districts, at desa Sampulungan Galesong to be precise.

Pantai Galesong is a great choice for family recreation, couples, community or even company gathering, training or celebrating anniversaries. There are many decent facilities such as spacious parking lot, decent guest houses with affordable prices, swimming pool for adults and children, ATV, moto cross, jetski, banana boat, traditional boat, outbond, cafes, etc.

For tourists who enjoy swimming in the sea could feel the warmth of the water in the morning. Life jacket will be provided there for rental. Visitors can also rent a traditional boat to explore the scenery of Pantai Galesong or even fishing.

Question and answer:

1. Where is Pantai Galesong located?

Answer: Pantai Galesong is located in Makassar, South Sulawesi.

2. How long does it take to reach there from Losari Beach?

Answer: You will need 30 minutes by car to reach the place.

3. What are facilities provided there?

Answer: Pantai Galesong provides decent facilities such as spacious parking lot, decent guest houses with affordable prices, swimming pool for adults and children, ATV, moto cross, jetski, banana boat, traditional boat, outbond, cafes, etc.

Generic structure dari descriptive text adalah:

Identification => Pendahuluan, yakni gambaran umum suatu topikDescription => Ciri-ciri tentang topik yang dibahas, sifat-sifat yang dimiliki, tampilan khas, dll.

26. contoh soal writing buat descriptive text!


║DESCRIPTIVE TEXT║

Answer

Here is the example of descriptive text about people.

My name is Dee. I have a lovely uncle. He is my mother's brother. He lives in French.  

My uncle is an independent man. He always does everything by himself. He has been working in French for 6 years by now. All of his friends love him because he is friendly. He is a handsome man. He has a pointed nose, short brown hair and thick eyebrows. His hobbies are swimming, playing badminton and eating all healthy foods. He loves exercising everyday. I'm proud of my uncle. Every time my visit to French, he always takes me to see Eiffel Tower and other beautiful places in French .

Explanation

DESCRIPTIVE TEXTGenre teks ini mendeskripsikan/menjabarkan informasi detail dari orang, tempat, hewan dan hal lain secara spesifik.  COMMUNICATIVE PURPOSE/SOCIAL FUNCTION to describe people/place/things specifically

{ Untuk mendeskripsikan orang/tempat/hal-hal secara khusus/spesifik }

GENERIC STRUCTURE

Identification

Bagian paragraf ini biasanya berada di awal sebagai pembukaan pengenalan orang/tempat/hal-hal yang dijabarkan

Description  

Bagian paragraf kedua berisi penjabaran karakteristik/ciri khas dari orang/tempat/hal-hal  yang dibicarakan.

Conclusion  

Bagian paragraf terakhir sebagai optional, bisa dipaparkan ataupun tidak. Bagian ini hanya berisi  informasi tambahan terkait dengan hal yang dibicarakan. Pada umumnya berupa ungkapan dari si penulis dari apa yang telah dijabarkan.

LANGUAGE FEATURE  ( Unsur Kebahasaan )

Penggunaan simple present tense

Dikarenakan penjabaran dari orang/tempat/hal-hal sifatnya nyata, berdasarkan fakta kebenaran, maka tenses yang digunakan adalah simple present tense baik dalam bentuk kalimat verbal dan nomina.

Penggunaan adjective ( kata sifat )

Dikarenakan yang dijabarkan adalah ciri khas / kharakteristik, maka, penggunaan adjective (kata sifat) lebih mendominasi.

Penggunaan linking verb ( relating verb )

Ini merupakan kata kerja pengikat subyek. Biasanya berupa BE present ( is, am,are, will, can , and etc ) sebagai pengikat kata sifat dan keterangan dari subyek yang dijabarkan.

Title: The Grand Canyon: A Natural Wonder

The Grand Canyon, located in Arizona, USA, is a geological marvel that captivates the imagination of visitors from all over the world. Carved by the mighty Colorado River over millions of years, this vast chasm stretches for 277 miles, reaches a width of 18 miles, and plummets to depths of over a mile. Its intricate and vibrant geology, with layers of colorful rock formations, narrates the Earth's history over millions of years. The Grand Canyon offers a unique opportunity for high school students to witness and study the fascinating world of geology.

The Grand Canyon's geological diversity is a highlight for budding geologists. Each rock layer, with its distinct colors and patterns, represents a different era in Earth's history. Students can explore these layers through hiking trails, such as the Bright Angel Trail, which takes them deep into the canyon. Helicopter tours offer a spectacular aerial view of the canyon's grandeur, providing a perspective that's equally educational and awe-inspiring.

This natural wonder is not only a geological treasure but also a habitat for diverse wildlife. Bighorn sheep, California condors, and a variety of plant species have adapted to the harsh desert environment of the Grand Canyon. For high school students interested in biology and ecology, observing these unique adaptations in the wild can be a transformative experience. The Grand Canyon is a living classroom where students can witness the interplay between nature and geology, making it an excellent destination for educational exploration.

Title: The Beautiful Borobudur Temple

Borobudur Temple is an ancient Buddhist temple located in Magelang, Central Java, Indonesia. It is one of the most famous and significant monuments in the country.

The temple was built during the Sailendra Dynasty in the 8th century. It was constructed using volcanic stone blocks and was designed in the shape of a mandala, representing the Buddhist cosmology. The temple consists of nine stacked platforms, with the top platform holding a large central dome.

Each platform is adorned with intricately carved reliefs that depict various scenes from Buddhist teachings and stories. The carvings showcase the exceptional craftsmanship of the ancient Javanese artisans. Visitors can spend hours marveling at the detailed artwork and trying to decipher the stories behind them.

One of the unique features of Borobudur Temple is its panoramic view. From the top platform, visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding lush green landscapes and distant mountains. It is truly a serene and tranquil experience.

Borobudur Temple is also a popular pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Every year, thousands of devotees come to the temple to perform religious rituals and seek spiritual enlightenment. The temple holds a special significance during Vesak, the celebration of Buddha’s birth, enlightenment, and passing.

In 1991, Borobudur Temple was recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage site, highlighting its cultural and historical importance. It attracts tourists from all around the globe, who come to witness its grandeur and immerse themselves in its rich history.

Visiting Borobudur Temple is not only a journey back in time but also an opportunity to appreciate the beauty of ancient architecture and the spiritual significance it holds. It is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in history, culture, and spirituality.

Descriptive Text

Descriptive text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu (manusia, hewan, tumbuhan dan benda) secara khusus atau spesifik.

Generic Structure dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Identification: Berisi tentang penjelasan singkat dari apa yang akan di deskripsikan.

2. Description: Mendeskripsikan benda, hean, tumbuhan dan manusia secara spesifik atau khusus.

Language features dari Descriptive text adalah:

1. Menggunakan simple present tense

2. Menggunakan kata yang spesifik atau tidak terlalu umum

3. Menggunakan adjective untuk mendeskripsikan sesuatu
