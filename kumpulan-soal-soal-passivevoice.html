9. •Andi plays football this evening•Rico played basketball yesterday•My Mother is buying some •food in the market•My father was reading a newspaper yesterday morningBantu kak,buatin kalimat pasif(passivevoice)​


Jawaban:

•Andi plays football this evening

Football is played by Andi this evening.

•Rico played basketball yesterday

Basketball was played by Rico yesterday.

•My Mother is buying some food in the market

Some food is being bought by my mother in the market.

•My father was reading a newspaper yesterday morning

A newspaper was being read by my father yesterday morning.

Penjelasan:

Passive voice :

present simple formula

is/am/are + verb 3

past simple formula

was/were + verb 3

present continuous formula

is/am/are + verb 3

past continuous formula

was/were + being + verb 3

future simple formula

will + be + verb 3

future be going to formula

is/am/are + being + verb 3

present perfect formula

have/has + been + verb 3

past perfect formula

had + been + verb 3

modal formula

modal + be + verb 3

modal perfect formula

modal + have + been + verb 3
