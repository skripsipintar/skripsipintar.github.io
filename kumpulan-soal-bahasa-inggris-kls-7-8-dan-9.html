15. tolong bantu kak soal bahasa inggris kls 8


Contoh No.1
Yesterday's temperature (is good), but today it (is better).
= Temperature kemarin (bagus), tapi hari ini (lebih bagus).

maka jawaban dalam kurung:
2. The morning's temperature (is bad), but at night it (is worse).
= temperature pagi (buruk), tapi pas malam (lebih buruk).

3. My hometown is Makasar. It (is far) from Surabaya. And it (is farther) from Jakarta.
= Kampung halamanku adalah Makasar. Itu jauh dari Surabaya. Dan itu (lebih jauh) dari Jakarta.

4. There (are many) people living in America, but there (are more many) people living in China.
= Ada (banyak) orang tinggal/hidup di Amerika, tapi ada (lebih banyak) orang tinggal/hidup di China.

5. There (is little) milk in the bottle, but there (is littler) milk in the refrigerator.
= Ada (sedikit) susu dalam botol, tapi ada (lebih sedikit) susu di kulkas

On Thursday, we visited the temples in Prambanan. There are three big temples, the Brahmana, Syiwa and Wisnu temples. They are really amazing. We visited only Brahmana and Syiwa temples, because Wisnu temple is being renovated.

a. the writer’s trip to Yogyakarta
b. the writer’s first visit to Prambanan
