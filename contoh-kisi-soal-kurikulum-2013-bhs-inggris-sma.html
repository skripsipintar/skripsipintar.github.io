3. jawaban soal bhs inggris paket hal 165 kelas x kurikulum 2013


Reported speech adalah kalimat tidak langsung yang diucapkan kembali oleh pembicara. Reported speech dalam penggunaannya mengalami perubahan tense dari kata-kata yang diucapkan secara langsung. Kata kerja yang digunakan dalam reported speech biasanya menggunakan kata kerja yang melaporkan (reporting verb) seperti say, tell, dan ask. Selain itu reported speech juga terkadang menggunakan kata that untuk memperkenalkan kata-kata yang dilaporkan.

Pembahasan

1. Issumboshi said to the Princess that he would stay in her pocket and guard her from all harm.

2. The Lord said that he would employ me.

3. Issumboshi told the Princess that he would defend her upon his life.

4. The Demon said that that magic hammer would grant me a wish.

5. The Demon said to Issumboshi that he gave that magic hammer to him.

6. John said that he was feeling ill.

7. Bob told me that Dan worked very hard.

8. Riana said that she didn't have any brothers or sisters.

9. Fadhil told me that Sarah had a very well-paid job.

10. Tika said that she didn't like fish.

---------------------------

INDIRECT SPEECH  

Indirect speech atau dikenal juga reported speech adalah kalimat tidak langsung yang diucapkan kembali oleh pembicara yang biasanya meggunakan perubahan tense dari kata-kata yang diucapkan secara langsung. Indirect speech biasanya menggunakan kata kerja yang melaporkan (reporting verb) seperti say, tell, dan ask. Selain itu indirect speech juga terkadang menggunakan kata that untuk memperkenalkan kata-kata yang dilaporkan. Hal lainnya yang perlu diperhatikan bahwa dalam kalimat indirect speech tidak menggunakan tanda petik. Berikut beberapa ketentuan yang harus diperhatikan dalam mengubah kalimat langsung (direct speech) menjadi kalimat tidak langsung (indirect speech), yaitu:  

1. Kalimat Pernyataan (Affirmative sentence)  

Kalimat pernyataan adalah kalimat yang berisi pernyataan seseorang baik dalam bentuk kalimat positif maupun dalam bentuk kalimat negatif. Contohnya:  

a. You have to take a bath twice a day.  

b. You don’t need to borrow her your books.  

Kedua kalimat tersebut adalah jenis kalimat pernyataan(Affirmative sentence). Jika kedua kalimat tersebut dirubah ke kalimat indirect/quoted speech dan diriect/reported speech, maka:  

a. Quoted Speech: Mother asked Fayyadh, “You have to take a bath twice        Reported Speech: Mother asked that Fayyadh had to take a bath  

twice a day.”  

b. Quoted speech: My friend said, “You don’t need to borrow her  

your books.”  

   Reported speech: My friend said that I didn’t need to borrow her  

my books.  

Maka pola perubahan kalimat pernyataan adalah sebagai berikut:  

S+V+THAT+S+V+O/C  

2. Kalimat Perintah (Command sentence)  

Kalimat perintah adalah kalimat yang berisi perintah/permintaan dari seseorang untuk orang lain melakukan sesuatu. Kalimat perintah ini juga dibagi dalam dua bentuk, yaitu kalimat perintah dalam bentuk kalimat positif dan kalimat perintah dalam bentuk kalimat negatif.  

Contohnya:  

a. Open the door!  

b. Don’t open the door!  

Jika kedua kalimat tersebut dirubah ke kalimat quoted speech dan reported speech, maka:  

a. Quoted Speech: Teacher said to student, “Open the door!”  

   Reported Speech: Teacher sait to student to open the door.  

b. Quoted speech: My friend said, “Don’t open the door!”  

    Reported speech: My freind said not to open the door.  

Maka pola perubahan kalimat perintah adalah sebagai berikut:  

Kalimat positif: S+V+TO VERB 1  

Kalimat negatif: S+V+NOT TO VERB 1  

3. Kalimat Pertanyaan (Question Sentence)  

Jenis kalimat pertanyaan dalam bahasa Inggris ada dua yaitu kalimat:  

1. Pertanyaan berupa jawaban ya atau tidak, atau lebih dikenal dengan YES/NO QUESTION.  

Contohnya: do you have any homework today?  

2. Pertanyaan yang jawabannya memberikan informasi lebih. Yaitu pertayaan yang menggunakan WH, yaitu what, why, when, where, dan how.  Jenis pertanyaan ini sering dikenal dengan WH-Question. Contohnya: when will it start?  

Jika kedua jenis kalimat pertanyaan tersebut dirubah ke kalimat quoted speech dan reported speech, maka:  

a. Quoted speech:Teacher asked students, “ do you have any  

homework today?”  

   Reported speeech: Teacher asked students if/whether they had  

any homework that day.  

b. Quoted speech: He said, “when will it start?”  

    Reported speech: He said when it would start.  

Maka pola perubahan kalimat pertanyaan adalah sebagai berikut:  

Kalimat yes/no question: S+V+IF/WHETHER+S+V  

Kalimat WH question: S+V+W/H+S+V

MENJAWAB PERTANYAAN TENTANG TANJUNG PUTING NATIONAL PARK  
Jawaban  Pendahuluan

TANJUNG PUTING NATIONAL PARK


One of the internationally famous ecotourism destinations in Indonesia is Tanjung Puting National Park in the southwest of Central Kalimantan peninsula. Tanjung Puting National Park offers impressive experience to ist visitors. This is called a park, but unlike any park that you have seen in your city, this is a jungle! It is a real jungle, which is home to the most incredible animals in the world: orang utans and proboscis monkeys! The male proboscis monkeys are interesting because  they have enermous snout. So, imagine yourself to be in the jungle and meet these special animals in their original habitat. What will you do when you meet them?


To see orang utans we should go to Camp Leakey . Camp Leakey is located in the heart of Tanjung Puting National Park. This is a rehabilitation place for ex-captive orang utans and also a prservation sit. This camp was established by Birute Galdikas, an important scientist who has studied orang utans since 1971.


To reach the place, we should take a boat down Sekonyet river. The boat is populary called perahu klotok which is a boathouse that can acommodate for people. The trip by the boat to Camp Leakey takes three days and two nights.  

The traveling in the boat offers anothr unforgettable experience. You sleep, cook, and eat in that klotok, night and day during your journey into the jungle. In daylight, on your way to camp Leakey, you can see trees filled with proboscis monkeys. At night, you can enjoy the clear sky and the amazingly bright stars as the only lights for the night.


Answer these questions!

1. Based on the text, can you guess what ecotourism is?


2. As on of ecotourism destination, what does Tanjung Puting National Park offer to tourists?


3. How is the park different from th parks in the cities?


4. How is Camp Leakey related to Tanjung Puting National Park?


5. How can people reach Camp Leakey?


6. What dos the word ex-captive tell you about the orang utans in Camp Leakey?


7. What is special about the means of transportation to Camp Leakey?


8. How interesting or uningteresting is the journey on the way to Camp Leakey? Why do you think so?


9. How interested are you in visiting Tanjung Puting National Park? What makes you interested (or not interested) in the park?


10. Give some examples of other ecotourism destinations that you know.

Pembahasan

1. Ecotourism is a tourism directed toward exotic, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife.

2. Tanjung Puting National Park offers the most incredible animals in

the world: orangutans and proboscis monkeys!

3. The difference between Tanjung Puting National park and the park in the cities is it's a real jungle.

4. Camp Leaky is located in the heart of Tanjung Puting National Park

5. To reach the place, we should take a boathouse down Sekonyer river.

6. The word ex-captive animal is an animal that captured by people and didn't live on its natural environment.

7. The traveling in the boat offers another unforgettable experience. We sleep, cook, and eat in that klotok, night and day during our journey into the jungle.

8. It's very interesting, because in daylight, on the way to Camp Leaky, we can see trees filled with proboscis monkeys. At night, we can enjoy the clear sky and the amazingly bright stars.

27. Buku Bhs Inggris kurikulum 2013 Kelas 8 halaman 56.Yg tau Boleh koment


Making request. Associationg sentence structure and completing the table. Buku Bahasa Inggris SMP kurikulum 2013 kelas 8 halaman 56.
JawabanPendahuluan

Untuk mengerjakan soal ini, kita harus memperhatikan contoh yang diberikan. Contoh pada tabel menunjukkan bahwa kita harus meletakkan kalimat-kalimat permintaan/perintah di atas ke dalam kotak-kotak yang tepat. Untuk menyelesaikan tugas ini, mungkin di halaman 54 tersedia beberapa kalimat lain yang mencantumkan nama Beni dan Edo. Karena keterbatasan soal, saya akan memberikan contoh berdasarkan kalimat yang tersedia.


Pembahasan

Seperti contoh yang diberikan, pada kotak pertama kita harus memasukkan nama atau subjek, kotak kedua harus diisi dengan kata yang menunjukkan perintah atau permintaan seperti please atau don’t. Kotak ketiga berisi kata kerja, kotak keempat berisi kata benda, dan kotak kelima berisi keterangan.


Contoh:

Don’t(2)eat(3)too much instant noodles(4).


Don’t adalah kata yang digunakan untuk perintah. Eat merupakan kata kerja. Too much instant noodle adalah kata benda.


Berikut adalah jawaban sesuai dengan tokoh-tokoh yang disebutkan pada lima kalimat yang tersedia. Kamu harus meletakkan bagian-bagian kalimat ke dalam kotak yang sesuai. Hal yang penting untuk diperhatikan adalah kalimat perintah dan saran yang tersedia.


Untuk mengerjakan tabel yang berisi Edo dan Beni, kamu dapat melihat kalimat-kalimat yang tersedia di halaman sebelumnya. Silahkan menggunakan jawaban ini untuk melengkapi tabel sesuai nama yang tertulis.

Siti: Please don’t(2)wear(3)a T-shirt(4).

Lina: Please(2)run, walk, or ride(3)a bicycle for exercise (4).

Lina: Don’t(2)use(3)expensive tools(4).

Mr.Adnan: Don’t(2)spill(3)paint(4)on the floor(5).

Mr.Adnan: Please(2)paint(3)the wall(4).

Mrs. Ina: Everybody(1)please(2)bring(3)your sewing kits(4)with you on Monday(5).

Pada exercise ini, siswa diminta untuk menuliskan hal-hal yang bisa dan tidak bisa dilakukan oleh tiga temannya di kelas. Saya disini akan memberikan contoh nama temannya adalah Paijo, Paijah dan Painah.

Paijo

Paijo can do many things. He can write English stories. He can answer  some difficult questions, he also can ask questions in English. He is not also good at  music. He cannot play the guitar. He cannot sing, too.  He can swim and run, but she cannot play football. He can take care of his garden.. He cannot cook..  His hobbies are gardening and running.

Paijah

Paijah can do many things. She can read English stories. She can follow and listen carefully to English movies without subtitle, She is  good at  music. She can play some musical instruments such as piano, guitar, drum, etc. Although she can play some musical instruments, she cannot sing.  She can  play badminton. She plays it regularly with her father. She can cook.. She can cook some Indonesian food such as fried rice, opor, rendang, etc. Her hobbies are cooking and playing music.

Painah

Painah can do many things. She can read and write English stories. She contributes in school wall magazine to write some interesting English stories. She is  good at  music. She can play play guitar and sing. Her favourite song is 'My heart will go on'. She is not good at sport. She cannot play badminton and basketball, but she usually do running in the morning. She can cook fried rice. she always cook fried rice whenever her classmates come to her house. She cannot take care of his family's garden. Her hobbies are writing and reading.

Pembahasan

Expressing ability (can) adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa seseorang mampu melakukan sesuatu. Untuk menuliskan expression ability, anda dapat mernggunakan form berikut ini:

S + can + verb 1

Contoh:

I can swim this river.

I can lift the table alone.

I can do some houseworks.

Expressing Inability (cannot) adalah kebalikan dari expressing ability. Expressing inability adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyatakan bahwa seseorang TIDAK MAMPU melakukan sesuatu. Untuk menuliskan expressing inability, anda dapat menggunakan form:

S + cannot + verb 1

Contoh:

She cannot swim.

He cannot lift the table alone.

She cannot do the houseworks.
