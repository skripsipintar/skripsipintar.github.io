5. buatlah dialog bahasa Inggris yang berisi kan tentang to get attentition, to chek if we are understood, to show appreciation, to tell what we or other people thinkjawab cepat pliss besok dikumpulin soalnya​


Penjelasan:

Sure! Here's a dialogue that includes getting attention, checking if we are understood, showing appreciation, and expressing opinions:

Characters:

Alex - Speaker

Lisa - Listener

Scenario: Alex and Lisa are colleagues working on a project together. They are discussing their ideas for an upcoming presentation.

Alex: (Tapping Lisa's shoulder) Hey, Lisa, can I run some ideas by you for the presentation?

Lisa: (Turning to Alex) Of course, Alex! I'm all ears. What do you have in mind?

Alex: (Explaining enthusiastically) I think we should start the presentation with a catchy hook to get attention. Maybe we could use a surprising statistic or a thought-provoking question.

Lisa: (Nods) That's a great idea! It will definitely grab the audience's attention right from the start.

Alex: (Smiling) Thanks for agreeing. Now, for the main content, we should break it down into three key points. What do you think?

Lisa: (Thoughtfully) That sounds like a solid structure. It will make it easier for the audience to follow along and understand our message.

Alex: (Relieved) I'm glad you think so. I wanted to make sure our ideas are clear and easy to grasp.

Lisa: (Smiling) You're doing a great job explaining everything. I'm following along perfectly.

Alex: (Appreciative) Thanks, Lisa. Your support means a lot to me.

Lisa: (Encouraging) No problem, Alex. We make a great team, and I believe our presentation will be fantastic!

Alex: (Showing appreciation) That's very kind of you to say. I value your input and creativity in this project.

Lisa: (Blushing) Thank you, Alex. I think your ideas are fantastic, too. We're on the right track!

Alex: (Excited) That's excellent to hear! Now, for the conclusion, I was thinking we could end with a powerful quote related to our topic.

Lisa: (Agreeing) Absolutely! A compelling quote will leave a lasting impression on the audience.

Alex: (Feeling satisfied) I'm happy we're on the same page. We've got a strong presentation plan now.

Lisa: (Supportive) Yes, we do! I'm confident that with our efforts, we'll make a positive impact during the presentation.

Alex: (Determined) Let's give it our best shot! Thank you, Lisa, for your valuable feedback and for being such an attentive listener.

Lisa: (Smiling) You're welcome, Alex. It's a pleasure working with you. Let's make this presentation unforgettable!

20. contoh 4 soal to check if we are understood


“Checking for understanding” adalah kalimat – kalimat yang bermaksud mengecek apakah lawan bicara sudah mengerti tujuan dan maksud kita. Contoh dialog “Checking for understanding” adalah sebagai berikut:



A: Hello, can you help me to find the way to go to X?


B: Sure. Are you taking the bus or on foot?


A: I will go by bus.


B: Ok, so from here, you can take bus number 601 in front of this building. And then you will transfer to bus 255 at M Station.


A: Yes. And after that? Where should I get off?


B: The final stop of bus 255 is N Station. You can get off there and walk about 15 minutes to X. Have you got it? Any question?


A: Yes, I got it. Thank you for your help.



Kalimat – kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk “checking for understanding” antara lain:


1. Do you know what I mean?


2. Do you know what I'm saying?


3. Do you understand?


4. Are you following me?


5. Are you with me (so far)?


6. Have you got it?


7. Any questions?
