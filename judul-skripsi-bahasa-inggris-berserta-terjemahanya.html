9. artikel bahasa inggris tentang solat dan terjemahanya


Sholat (Prayer)
Sholat is an arabic or Quranic term which means to worship to God (Allah) in Islam. It’s different with other religion prayers.
Before doing sholat, a muslim should take wudlu, he or she washes his or her certain parts of body such as face, hands, feet, etc. Then we can do sholat.
There are two kinds of sholat, they are obligatory and sunnah prayers. Obligatory prayer means we musto do them otherwise we will get sin. Sunnah means it can be done or not, but preferably, we can do it to get additional reward from Allah.
Islam stands on five obligatory prayers. They are; Isya’, Shubuh, Luhur(Dzuhur), Ashar, and Magrib. The all prayers can be abbreviated with “Islam”. That’s amazing. So, if we leave even one prayer of the five, we are not called “Muslim”, it means one who does the five obligatory prayers like the abbreviation mentioned above.
Sholat (Doa)
Sholat adalah istilah Arab atau Quran yang berarti menyembah kepada Allah (Allah) dalam Islam. Ini berbeda dengan doa agama lainnya.
Sebelum melakukan sholat, seorang muslim harus mengambil wudlu, dia mencuci bagian-bagian tubuhnya yang tertentu seperti wajah, tangan, kaki, dll. Lalu kita bisa melakukan sholat.
Ada dua jenis sholat, yaitu shalat wajib dan sunnah. Doa wajib berarti kita harus melakukannya jika tidak kita akan mendapatkan dosa. Sunnah berarti itu bisa dilakukan atau tidak, tetapi sebaiknya, kita bisa melakukannya untuk mendapatkan pahala tambahan dari Allah.
Islam berdiri di lima doa wajib. Mereka; Isya ’, Shubuh, Luhur (Dzuhur), Ashar, dan Magrib. Semua doa bisa disingkat dengan "Islam". Itu luar biasa. Jadi, jika kita meninggalkan bahkan satu shalat dari lima orang itu, kita tidak disebut "Muslim", itu berarti seseorang yang melakukan lima sholat wajib seperti singkatan yang disebutkan di atas.Sholat is mean how to syukur to our god.We pray 5 times in a day.

11. Diskripsi tentang ikan ,beserta bahasa inggris nya


English:
Fish are aquatic animals which belong to the group of vertebrates. There are more than 30.000 species of fish in the world. Some of them live in freshwater and some other live in an ocean. All of them breathe by using a special organs known as “gills”. They consume various of food such as plants, worms, and sometimes another fish with smaller body.

Each species of fish have their own maximum measurement. It can range from 8 mm to 16 m. Most of them have scaly skin with various colors. Some of them even have a unique pattern on their scales which makes them more valuable for humans as a decorative items. They have some sets of muscles on their backbone which allow them to move their body and create ripples in the water that will push their body forward so they can swim. Each of them also have some fins on their body to navigate their movement. Many of them have an internal organ called as “swim bladder” which contains gases that prevent them from sinking.

Fish contain a lot of good nutrient for the body such as protein, omega 3 and vitamine. Since a long time ago, some species of fish, especially the freshwater fish, has been breed in a fish farm in order to fulfill the needs of human. Beside that, some fish such as Koi Fish and Betta Fish are famous for their beauty and they are kept in a pond or in an aquarium as a decoration.

Tukar ke Inggris

Ikan adalah hewan air yang termasuk dalam kelompok vertebrata. Ada lebih dari 30.000 spesies ikan di dunia. Beberapa dari mereka hidup di air tawar dan beberapa lainnya hidup di lautan. Mereka semua bernafas dengan menggunakan organ khusus yang dikenal sebagai "insang". Mereka mengonsumsi berbagai makanan seperti tanaman, cacing, dan terkadang ikan lain dengan tubuh lebih kecil.

Setiap spesies ikan memiliki ukuran maksimum sendiri. Dapat berkisar dari 8 mm hingga 16 m. Kebanyakan dari mereka memiliki kulit bersisik dengan berbagai warna. Beberapa dari mereka bahkan memiliki pola unik pada sisik mereka yang menjadikannya lebih berharga bagi manusia sebagai barang dekoratif. Mereka memiliki beberapa set otot di tulang belakang mereka yang memungkinkan mereka untuk menggerakkan tubuh mereka dan membuat riak di air yang akan mendorong tubuh mereka ke depan sehingga mereka bisa berenang. Masing-masing dari mereka juga memiliki beberapa sirip di tubuh mereka untuk menavigasi gerakan mereka. Banyak dari mereka memiliki organ internal yang disebut "kantong renang" yang mengandung gas yang mencegah mereka tenggelam.

 

Ikan mengandung banyak nutrisi yang baik untuk tubuh seperti protein, omega 3 dan vitamin. Sejak dahulu kala, beberapa spesies ikan, terutama ikan air tawar, telah dikembangbiakkan di sebuah peternakan ikan untuk memenuhi kebutuhan manusia. Selain itu, beberapa ikan seperti Ikan Koi dan Ikan Betta terkenal karena kecantikannya dan disimpan di kolam atau di akuarium sebagai hiasan.

14. Contoh Story Telling Singkat/Pendek Dalam Bahasa Inggris Beserta Terjemahanya


The Lonely Landy
One day, there was a porcupine named Landy. He was lonely. No one wanted to play with him, because they were afraid of his spike.
“Dear Landy. We don’t want to play with you because your spikes are too sharp. We don’t want you to hurts us,” said Cici the rabbit one day.
“Cici is right, Landy. It is not because you’re bad or rude to us, no, Landy. Just because of your spikes. They will stab us if we close to you,” said Tito the rooster.
Landy felt lonely. Landy spent most of the time day dreaming at the river bank, “I would be happy if there were no spikes on my body.”
Suddenly, Kuku the turtle appeared from the river. He came to Landy and said, “Landy, what are you thinking of?”
“Oh, nothing,” Landy replied.
“Don’t lie to me, Landy! Who knows I can help you,” said Kuku wisely. Then he sat beside Landy. He wasn’t afraid of Landy’s spikes.
Shortly, Landy told his problem. Kuku nodded his head. He said, “Poor you. But it isn’t your fault. I know, your spikes are very useful and helpful for you. They will realize it someday. Trust me!”
“Thanks, Kuku. You are my best friend.”
One day, Koko the frog held his birthday party. He invited all his friends, including Landy. But he decided not to come. He didn’t want to mess up the party.
“I’ll come with you Landy. I’ll tell everyone that you’re harmless,” said Kuku. Finally Landy attended the party. Everyone enjoyed it.
Suddenly Tito screamed, “Help….help.…! The evil wolf is coming. Save yourself!” Then, everyone saved their lives, except Kuku and landy. Kuku pulled his head and his leg into his shell. And Landy rolled his body into a ball.
Unintentionally, the evil wolf touched Landy. Of course, the spike pricked him. He screamed, “Ouch!” Since his foot was bleeding, he didn’t chase Landy’s friends any longer. Then, he ran away.
“Horray….horray….! Long live Landy! He saved our lives,” said Cici and her friends. They thanked him from then on. Landy wasn’t lonely anymore.

Ants are small insects. All insects have six legs and three parts to their bodies–the head, the thorax, and the abdomen. Some ants have a stinger. They all have two antennae.
